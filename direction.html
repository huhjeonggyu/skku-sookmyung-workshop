Destinations

Sookmyung Women's University – Department of Mathematics
100, Cheongpa-ro 47-gil, Yongsan-gu, Seoul 04310

Closest stations: Line 4 Sookmyung Univ. (Galwol) or Line 1 Namyeong.

Hotel (Conference lodging): Shilla Stay Mapo
83, Mapo-daero, Mapo-gu, Seoul (near Gongdeok Station on AREX / Lines 5 & 6)

1) From the Airports → Shilla Stay Mapo (Hotel)

From Incheon (ICN): AREX → Gongdeok

~50–60 min ~₩4,500–5,500
  1. Take AREX (all-stop or express) to Gongdeok Station.
  2. Follow exit signs toward Shilla Stay Mapo (Exit 1); walk ~3–7 min (hotel just off Mapo-daero).

Express may require transfer at Seoul Station depending on schedule.

From Gimpo (GMP): AREX → Gongdeok

~15–25 min ~₩1,450–2,000
  1. From Gimpo Airport Station, take AREX to Gongdeok (few stops).
  2. Walk ~3–7 min to the hotel following station signage (Exit 1).

Alternatively, Line 5 or Line 6 also serve Gongdeok.

Taxi

ICN ~₩55,000–85,000 · 45–70 min GMP ~₩20,000–35,000 · 20–40 min

Time and fare vary by traffic/peak hours.

2) Shilla Stay Mapo ⇄ Sookmyung (Math Dept)

Subway (fastest, 2 transfers or fewer)

~15–25 min ~₩1,450
  1. From Gongdeok Station (Exit 1), take Line 6 toward Hyochang Park (1 stop).
  2. From Hyochang Park Station (Exit 2), walk ~12–15 min or take a short local bus to Sookmyung Univ. (Galwol) area.
  3. Exit 10 and walk to campus (5–10 min to Math Dept within campus).

Alternatively: AREX to Seoul Station → Line 4 to Sookmyung (1 stop).

Taxi

~10–20 min ~₩8,000–15,000

Useful when carrying posters or luggage; traffic near central Seoul may add time.
